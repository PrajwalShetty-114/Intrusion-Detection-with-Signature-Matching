Project Objectives

  • To study the fundamental principles of network-based Intrusion Detection Systems.
  • To understand the "signature-based" matching model as a method for threat detection.
  • To design and develop a web-based simulation that implements the signature matching algorithm.
  • To create a user-friendly interface where users can input "network traffic" and observe the IDS detection process.
  • To clearly demonstrate the system's response when a known threat signature is detected versus when traffic is benign.
